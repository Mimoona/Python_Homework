Excercises:
1. Create a string a = “hello world”.

a) Find out what a.endswith("world") is doing. Formulate generally what
string1.endswith(string2) is doing.

b) Find out what a.startswith("world") is doing. Formulate generally
what string1.startswith(string2) is doing.

2. Create a string str1 = "I love Python"
a) Find out what str1.lower() is doing. Formulate generally what str.lower() is doing.
b) Find out what str1.upper() is doing. Formulate generally what str.upper() is doing.
c) Find out what str1.swapcase() is doing. Formulate generally what str.swapcase() is doing.
